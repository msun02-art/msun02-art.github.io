About

Mandy Sun

BFA, Rhode Island School of Design (RISD), Class of 2028

Major: Art and Computation

Work spans drawing/painting and computational approaches, with an emphasis on storytelling, character-based worldbuilding, and interactive systems.

Current interests include generative thinking, digital materiality, and the emotional texture of interfaces—how images, systems, and narratives shape perception and memory.
